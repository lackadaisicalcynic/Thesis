[High-grade prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia: state-of-the-art].  According to current views, high-grade prostatic intraepithelial neoplasia is the most likely precursor of prostate adenocarcinoma.
This review gives the latest data of genetic, proteomic, and morphological analyses of this neoplasia and touches upon the probems that might arise when searching for new markers for differential diagnosis and prognosis estimation.
