[Cell therapy for ischemic stroke.  Stem cell types and results of pre-clinical trials].
The literature review addresses the use of stem cells (SC) in ischemic stroke (IS).  Part 1 of the paper overviews the results of experimental animal studies.
Characteristics of different SC types and results of their studies in experimental models of IS are presented in the first section, the second section considers pros and cons of the methods of SC injection.
