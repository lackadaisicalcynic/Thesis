[Effect of mouth breathing on the severity of morphological and functional changes of dental system in patients with otolaryngologic pathology].
The study included 282 patients aged 5 to 14 years with pathology of the pharyngeal tonsil.  Diagnostic algorithm is presented.
The study results allowed elaboration of complex rehabilitation of nasal breathing and miodynamic equilibrium in the maxillofacial region by means of elastopositioner.
