[Endoscopic surgery in the treatment of patients with extensive odontogenic cysts].  The aim of the study was the assessment of effectiveness of endoscopic techniques in the treatment of extensive odontogenic cysts.
Endosurgery for diagnostic and therapeutic purposes was used in 67 patients with odontogenic cysts of the jaws: 23 follicular cysts, 19 radicular cysts, 6 residual cysts, and 19 keratokists.
The results prove that the developed methods of endovideosurgery of odontogenic cysts have low invasiveness, provide an optimal healing of bone tissue and reduce postoperative complications.
