[Malabsorption is a leading clinical sign of small bowel disease].  The paper presents a variety of clinical manifestations of malabsorption syndrome (MAS) in celiac disease, collagenous sprue, Whipple's disease, Crohn's disease, intestinal lymphangiectasia, amyloidosis, common variable immune deficiency, and treatment of short bowel syndrome.
It shows the specific features of the pathophysiology, diagnosis, and treatment of MAS in small bowel diseases.
