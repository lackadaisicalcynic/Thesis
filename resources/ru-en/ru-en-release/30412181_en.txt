[The peri-lymphatic labyrinthine fistula in a child].  The objective of the present work was to describe the successful surgical treatment of the peri-lymphatic round window fistula of the labyrinth in the 9 year old child.
The authors briefly mention the difficulties of diagnostics of the peri-lymphatic labyrinthine fistulas in the children and the signs suggesting their presence with special reference to the optimal strategies for their treatment.
