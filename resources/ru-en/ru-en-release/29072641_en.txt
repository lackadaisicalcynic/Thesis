[Manifestation of Crohn's disease in the oral cavity: clinical case].  The article contains the review of possible oral manifestations of Crohn's disease and clinical case presentation of purulent alveolitis resistant to conventional local treatment, geographical glossitis and xerostomia in 42 y.o. female with the history of periodical digestive disorders, diagnosed with Crohn's disease.
The diagnosis was based on abovementioned oral manifestations.
