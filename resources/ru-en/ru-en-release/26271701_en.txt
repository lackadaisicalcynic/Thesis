[Visual perception of dentition esthetic parameters (part 1)].  The article presents a study on the impact of violations of aesthetic parameters as the inclination of the incisalline, thedislocation of median interincisal line and the width of dental arch on visual perception.
Comparison of the data of objective assessment data the subjective assessment of the respondents was conducted.
It is proved that all dependencies are linear and can be described by a linear regression equations.
