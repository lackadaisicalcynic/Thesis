[Sexual dysfunction in men with depressive disorders].  In this article, the authors summarize literature data about sexual dysfunction in depression in men.
Prevalence, clinical issues and pathogenesis of sexual dysfunction in patients with affective disorders are described.
The incidence of depression is steadily growing, and the structure of depressive disorders is becoming more complicated.
Comorbid sexual disturbances continued to be one of not enough developed aspects of depressive disorders.
