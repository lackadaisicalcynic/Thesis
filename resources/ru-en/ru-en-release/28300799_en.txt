[The case of completed pregnancy after hemorrhagic stroke in the patient with moyamoya disease].  Moya-moya syndrome is a rare chronic genetic disease characterized by progressive cerebral vascular occlusion and progressive stenosis of inner carotid arteries resulting in the frequent ischemic attacks and development of collateral arterial circulation.
Pregnant patients with moya-moya syndrome are at high risk due to the development of complications.
The authors present a case report of full-term pregnancy in a woman with moya-moya syndrome.
