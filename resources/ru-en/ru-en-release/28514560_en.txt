[Organization of beds nursing care as a form of implementation of a comprehensive strategy for health and social care elderly people].  The article tells about one of the actual problem of the provision of medical and social care for elderly and old aged people, through the organization of nursing care beds in the Voronezh region.
Since the beginning of 2014 there were organized 320 such beds in 27 medical organizations of the region.
Regular surveys of patients and their relatives, as well as personnel involved in the care service, made it possible to make the necessary adjustments and the further expansion of services in other hospitals.
Thus the end of 2014 the number of nursing beds has been increased to 525, so more than a thousand patients have received the necessary nursing care.
