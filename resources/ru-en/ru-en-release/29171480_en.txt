[Ferrous sulfate in the treatment of iron deficiency anemia: The positions continue].
The paper discusses treatment strategy and tactics for iron deficiency anemia.  It gives data on the comparative efficacy of different iron sulfate drugs, their bioavailability, effects on peroxidation processes, and side effects.
The paper also considers the clinical significance of a dosage form of iron-containing drugs with a sustained iron release, as well as ways to reduce the frequency and magnitude of side effects when ferrous sulfate is used.
