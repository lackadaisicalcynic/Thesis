Dental treatment before planned pregnancy was, however, completed in only half of the patients.  When choosing materials and constructions for oral rehabilitation the leading criteria for men were 'quality' and 'safety', while women chose 'esthetics' and 'quality'.
Both genders would like to have their teeth 'whiter' but women undergo bleaching procedures more often.  The dentists need to consider the revealed gender-related features for planning of prevention, treatment and full oral rehabilitation.
