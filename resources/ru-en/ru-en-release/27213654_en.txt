[The post-streptococcal diseases in the clinical practice].  The authors describe various clinical forms of streptococcal infections, the pathogenetic mechanisms underlying these conditions, and peculiarities of their clinical picture.
Also considered are the modern methods employed for laboratory diagnostics of streptococcal infections.
The probability of the risk of development of post-streptococcal diseases in the children is evaluated.  Measures for the prevention of these conditions are proposed.
