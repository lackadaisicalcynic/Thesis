[Sleep disorders in the structure of cerebrovascular diseases].  The literature on the place and role of insomnia in cerebral blood circulation disturbances is reviewed.
It is emphasized that insomnia is a modifying risk factor of cerebrovascular pathology.
The syndrome of obstructive sleep apnea may be a cause of arterial hypertension.
The diagnostic relevance of the complex examination of patients with sleep pathology, including polysomnographic technology, and its role in the choice of corrective measures and treatment of insomnia in whole are shown.
