[Quantitative and qualitative analysis of oral microbiota by orthopedic rehabilitation with full and partial removable dentures].  Microbiological analysis of biomaterial surface in dental prosthesis showed the most common colonizing gram-positive species to be S. aureus, Micrococcus spp., S. haemolyticus, E. faecalis, mainly massive colonization with S. aureus was seen.
The highest concentration of C. albicans colonization was found in removable dentures and may have a destructive effect on prosthetic material.
