[Eponyms related to Nazism neurosciences].  The author considers eponymous names of CNS diseases related to physicians who had actively cooperated with Nazis during the Second World War.
The data on the activity of some specialists in the field of neurosciences whose activity did not correspond to ethical values of the physician and the scientist are presented.
The author suggests excluding a number of eponymous terms associated with the names of physicians, former Nazis or those who actively cooperated with Nazis.
