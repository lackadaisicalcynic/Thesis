[A patient with severe idiopathic pulmonary arterial hypertension: Is there a way out?]  The paper considers a clinical example of current therapy for idiopathic pulmonary arterial hypertension in a female patient with its clinical deterioration.
It shows the tactics of switching from one drug (bosentan) to others (macitentan) within in the same pathogenetic therapy group (endothelin receptor antagonists).
The latest pulmonary hypertension classification (Nice, 2013) is presented.  The current strategies of pathogenetic therapy for pulmonary hypertension are discussed.
