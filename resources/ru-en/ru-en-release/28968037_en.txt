[Pharmaceutical care for patients of the third age in pharmacy organizations as a significant aspect of social gerontology].
There are questions in the article that study mutual relations of patients of elderly and senile age with pharmaceutical experts.  It was revealed that pharmacy organizations are an important element in the life of patients of the third age and noted the high role of the pharmaceutical specialist in their social environment.
The article shows the need to focus on the psychosomatic features of elderly patients with proper pharmaceutical counseling and professional communication.
It was noted that interaction of patients of the third age with pharmaceutical specialists and their confidence in them has high importance.
