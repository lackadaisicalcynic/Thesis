[30th Annual Meeting of the European Society for Vascular Surgery (Copenhagen, Denmark, september 28-30, 2016)].
The article is a brief review of the proceedings of the 30th Annual Meeting of the European Society for Vascular Surgeons, held on September 28-30, 2016 in Copenhagen, Denmark.
The papers presented reported the results of managing patients with pathology of carotid arteries, aortic aneurysms, pathology of peripheral arteries, also discussing current achievements and prospects of methods of treatment of arterial and venous diseases.
