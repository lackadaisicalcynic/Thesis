[Anesthesia during video-assisted thoracoscopic lobectomy using laryngeal ductwork and auxiliary mechanical ventilation].  The article presents the experience of video-assisted thoracoscopic lobectomies in patients without endotracheal intubation and sparing of spontaneous breathing.
The results of intraoperative monitoring and laboratory data of all perioperative period are described.  In view of these data it was concluded that such approach can decline the severity of stress-response against surgical aggression in this group of patients.
