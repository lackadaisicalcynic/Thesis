[Zero citation of Russian institute publications on the psychiatry and addiction].
AIM
To evaluate the zero-citation sizes.
MATERIAL AND METHODS  Based on the data of the Russian Science Citation Index the publication activity of four leading institutes in the field of Psychiatry and Addiction is analyzed.
The same indices in the field of Neurology were used as a control.  Number and percentage of publications with â‰¥1 citations and zero-citation were analyzed.
RESULTS  It has been shown that in psychiatric science zero citation rate is quite high (from 32.8% to 47.2%, an average of 42.9%).
It is higher compared to the control.
CONCLUSION  Zero-citation indicator is essential to evaluate the effectiveness of scientific institutions.
