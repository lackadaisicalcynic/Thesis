[Pramipexole in Parkinson's disease].  Pramipexole is non-ergoline dopamine receptor agonist.
There is accumulating evidence for the efficacy of pramipexole in treatment of Parkinson's disease (PD).
Authors have summarized the results concerning the optimal start treatment, the using of pramipexole in early and advanced PD stages, effects of pramipexole on tremor, cognitive impairment, affective functions and safety pramipexole.
