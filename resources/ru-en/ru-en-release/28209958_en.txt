[Surgical treatment of bicondylar tibial fractures].
AIM
To improve surgical treatment of bicondylar tibial fractures.  MATERIAL AND METHODS
Outcomes of 69 victims were analyzed.  Two groups were compared to assess an efficacy of surgical techiques (two-stage surgery, osteosynthesis under joint distraction, combination of new L-shaped external and L-shaped internal approaches, osteoplasty with b-tricalcium phosphate, carbon nanostructured implant and xenoplastic material "Osteomatrix").
CONCLUSION
Efficacy of treatment was higher in the main group compared with control group (50 and 27.6% respectively).
