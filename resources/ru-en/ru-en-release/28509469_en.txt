[The review of the peptides used in dermatocosmetology].
The review of the peptides used in dermatocosmetology is represented.  The purpose of this review is a search of results of the basic scientific researches confirming or disproving efficiency and/or safety of peptide molecules used in a modern dermatocosmetology.
Questions of forms of application, biological availability, reliability of the rendered anti-aging effects are considered.
