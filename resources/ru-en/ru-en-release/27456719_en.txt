[A psychovegetative symptom complex in the structure of didactogenic depression].  AIM
To study clinical characteristics of didactogenic depression.
MATERIAL AND METHODS  Eighty elementary and middle school students with psychogenic didactogenic depression were studied.
RESULTS AND CONCLUSION
The following variants of didactogenic depression were described: timid, with «intellectual collapse», behavioral disturbances and with psychosomatic masking.
Psychovegetative disturbances emerge to varying degrees in all variants.
New features of somatic/autonomic symptoms in didactogenic depression and organization of psychocorrection are presented.
