[The investigation into specific features of the distribution of carbofuran in the body of the warm-blooded animals].
The objective of the present study was to estimate the distribution of the carbamate pesticide carfbofuran in the organism of the warm-blooded animals (rats) after its intragastric administration.
It was shown that large amounts of unmetabolized carbofuran were present in the internal organs and biological fluids.  The highest content of carbofuran was recorded in the stomach, large and small intestines, testicles, urinary bladder, ureters, and heart.
