[Cerebral actinomycotic granuloma mimicking malignant brain tumor].  We report a clinical case of cerebral actinomycotic granuloma that was preoperatively diagnosed as an intracerebral tumor.
On the basis of a histological examination performed after lesion resection, the diagnosis of a cerebral actinomycotic granuloma was made.
The article presents long-term outcomes of the surgery.
The literature data on cases of various forms of cerebral actinomycosis are provided.
