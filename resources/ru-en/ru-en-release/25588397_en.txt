[New method of conduction anesthesia in the maxilla].  There was the research aimed at improving the effeciency of intraosseous anesthesia in the maxilla by blocking the infraorbital nerve conduction along its entire length.
In the experimental part of the needle puncture defined place and character of the spreading of contrast medium into the upper jaw.
In the clinical part of the study shows the advantages of the proposed method of intraosseous anesthesia.
