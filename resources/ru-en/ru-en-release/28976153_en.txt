[TREATMENT OF CHEMICAL STOMATITIS IN PEOPLE OF ADVANCED AND SENILE AGE].  The author presents the results of the surveillance of medical rehabilitation of 22 elderly patients suffering from chemical stomatitis combined with medication sialadenopathys that occurred after combined treatment of malignant tumors of oropharyngeal area.
It is shown that the inclusion in the conventional regimen chemical stomatitis peptide bioregulator Vezugen and dietary supplement Algiklam enhances the effectiveness of the treatment gerontostomatologic patients suffering from chemical stomatitis combined with medication sialadenopathys.
