Part I: Memory clinics and Alzheimer's disease centers].  The review of publications on an influence of demographic changes in the population on the prevalence of old age dementias and a scale of burden of dementias in modern society are presented.
This paper is the first part of the review.  It presents the necessity of new forms of out-patient care to the aged with cognitive impairment.
The author reviews the history of creation of memory clinics and Alzheimer's disease centers.
