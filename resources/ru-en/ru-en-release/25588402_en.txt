[Impact of various second-stage implant surgery methods on adjacent gingival tissues microcirculation].  The paper presents the rezults of laser doppler flowmetry (LDF) studies of microcirculation in gingival tissues around dental implants disclosed by different methods.
Microhemodynamics in gingival tissues when using a scalpel recovered after 14 days, carbon dioxide laser -- 7 days and erbium laser -- 3 days, which may be important in the choice of the operation tactics.
