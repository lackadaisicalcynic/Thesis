[Enteric microbiocenotic modifiers and ways of enhancing their efficacy].  Efficient recovery of enteric microbiocenosis is one of the central problems not only in gastroenterology, but also in a number of related areas.
This problem becomes particularly pressing after antibiotic treatment.
The combined modifiers of enteric microbiocenosis are most effective among other symbiotic drugs.
Bion-3 apart from symbionts contains a vitamin/mineral complex and acts as an adaptogen.
