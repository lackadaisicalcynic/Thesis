[Organization of quality control of the forensic medical investigations].  The article describes the system of quality control at all stages of forensic chemical investigations (including pre-analytical, analytical, and post-analytical ones) carried out by the Saint-Petersburg Bureau of Forensic Medical Expertise It is shown that standardization and control taken together make up a solid foundation for obtaining the reliable and reproducible results of forensic chemical investigations.
Responsibility for the quality control at all stages of forensic chemical investigations lies on the heads of the respective departments.  Special staff members are appointed to supervise performing each concrete form of control and keeping the relevant documentation.
