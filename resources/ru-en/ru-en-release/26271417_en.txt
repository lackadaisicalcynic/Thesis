[Surgical risks and their prevention in laparoscopic cholecystectomy].
It was analyzed the treatment results of 3739 patients with chronic and acute cholecystitis who underwent laparoscopic cholecystectomy.
Three groups of predisposing factors were determined in 427 high risk patients.  Laparoscopic cholecystectomy in view of these factors and enhancement of approach to dissect gall-bladder decreases the number of intraoperative complications.
