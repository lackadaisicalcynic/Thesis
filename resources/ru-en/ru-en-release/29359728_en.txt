[Gut human microbiota and multiple sclerosis].  Recently the relationship between gut microbiota changes and the development of immune-mediated diseases of the central nervous system (CNS) has been reported.
This review presents literature data on the effect of gut microbiota on the function of the immune and nervous systems.
The authors discuss possible mechanisms of the relationship between gut microbiota changes and CNS diseases on the model of multiple sclerosis (MS).
