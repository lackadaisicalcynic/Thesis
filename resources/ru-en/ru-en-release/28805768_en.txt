[Organization of out-patient psychiatric care in dementia and cognitive impairment in aged.  Part II: Clinical and economic efficacy of memory clinics and Alzheimer's disease centers].  The part II of the review is focused on a history of developing of memory clinics and Alzheimer's disease centers as well as on the indices of their activity in various countries and in Russia.
Approaches to the evaluation of clinical and economic efficacy of new technologies of organization of care and a role of the national programs in solving of the problem of old age dementias were considered.
