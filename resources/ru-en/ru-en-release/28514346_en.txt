[Systemic toxicity of local anesthetics].  The paper presents characteristics of local anesthetics used in dentistry and maxillofacial surgery taking into account their effectiveness and toxicity.
We described the main clinical symptoms of manifestation of systemic toxicity and measures for prevention of local anesthesia complications, as well as the detailed protocol of 'lipid rescue' and resuscitation at manifestation of systemic toxic reaction of local anesthetics with the mechanism of the fatty emulsion action.
