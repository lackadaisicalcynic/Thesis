[Surgical management of traumatic retroperitoneal hemor-rhage].  AIM
To present the algorithm of management of victims using modern technologies including CT, laparoscopy, angiography, intraabdominal pressure monitoring, etc.  MATERIAL AND METHODS
Treatment of 395 victims with traumatic retroperitoneal hemorrhage was analyzed.
RESULTS  It was shown that the use of developed algorithm decreases mortality rate from 10.5 to 4.5% and postoperative complications incidence from 23 to 14.1% in reviewed patients.
