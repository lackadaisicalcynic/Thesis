[Staged reconstruction of brachiocephalic arteries and coronary artery bypass grafting].  It is presented the results of staged treatment of 28 patients with lesion of brachiocephalic arteries and ischemic heart disease.
Patients underwent reconstructive surgery on brachiocephalic arteries (n=34) and coronary artery bypass grafting (n=28).
Diagnostic and surgical features are described and indications for this technique are defined.
The authors studied Russian and international experience, described postoperative complications and suggested ways to improve the results of simultaneous interventions.
