[Tympanophonia as a topical problem of practical medicine].
This paper contains the data suggesting the necessity and importance of the comprehensive treatment of tympanophonia on an individual basis including the rational choice of the combination of pharmacotherapeutic and non-medicamental modalities, such as classical acupuncture and the like.
The special emphasis is laid on the importance of compliance with the prescribed hygienic procedures for the organs of hearing and prophylaxis of cochlear disorders.
