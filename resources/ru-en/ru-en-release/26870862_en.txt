[The modern strategy for the treatment of the patients presenting with odontogenic maxillary sinusitis and the oroantral fistula].  The objective of the present study was the comparative analysis of various methods for the plastic correction of the oroantral fistula with the use of the mucosal flap and the osteoplastic materials based on the data from the literature and on-line publkations.
The characteristics of an ideal material for the plastic correction ot the oroantral fistula and the conditions for carrying out this surgery are discussed.
