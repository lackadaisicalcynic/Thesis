[The multidisciplinary approach to the diagnostics and surgical treatment of the lacrimal passages].  The primary objective of the present study was to highlight the current state of research on the pathology of the lacrimal organs based on the results of the analysis of the relevant publications in the domestic and foreign scientific literature.
Special attention in this review is given to the problems of diagnostics, indications for the probing, the treatment and stenting strategies.
The authors report their original observations contributing to the better understanding of the anatomical features of the nasolacrimal passages.  In addition, the data on the principal pathogenic agents are presented together with certain peculiarities of the surgical treatment of the pathology under consideration.
