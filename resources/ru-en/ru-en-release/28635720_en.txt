[Poststroke depression - a common medical and social problem].
Poststroke depression (PD) is considered as a common medical and social problem.
Depression is estimated to occur in 30-35% of the patients during the first year after stroke.
Despite the high level of comorbidity, PD is frequently undetectedand and untreated.  Psychological impairment from stroke has a negative effect on functional outcome, rehabilitation and quality of live.
On the basis of literature review present prevalence, etiology, pathogenesis,diagnosis of PD and summarize current recommendations for therapeutic intervention.
A rational approach to the treatment and prevention of PD is proposed.
