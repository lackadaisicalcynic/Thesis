[Administration of Neovasculgen agent for treatment of a patient with neuroischaemic form of diabetic foot syndrome].  Presented herein is a case report concerning successful treatment of a patient with the neuroischaemic form of diabetic foot syndrome using Neovasculgen(®) agent.
This case by salvage of the supporting leg could have been considered unpromising without the use of the methods of therapeutic angiogenesis.
The use of Neovasculgen(®) made it possible to obtain an optimal result with rehabilitation of the patient and preserving the initial level of his quality of life.
