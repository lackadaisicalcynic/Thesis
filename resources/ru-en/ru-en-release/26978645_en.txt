UNLABELLED  Research objective - studying of results of a comprehensive neuro-rehabilitation patients in the acute ischemic stroke (according to the Samara regional vascular centre).
MATERIAL AND METHODS
A review of the work of RVC on the main indicators for 2014, detailed analysis of 20 patients in the acute period of ischemic stroke, in which treatment was used the drug mexidol with the dynamics on standardized assessment scales (NIHSS, Rankin, Rivermead, MoCA, HADS).
RESULTS AND DISCUSSION
The use of «Mexidol» in the treatment of stroke leads to a significant statistically and clinically significant improvement in cognitive, motor, sensory functions, reduction of fatigue, anxiety, improved adaptation to physical loads.
