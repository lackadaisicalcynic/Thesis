[Influence of changes in the reproductive behavior on the average duration of the reproductive period and life span].  This article explains the model of changing the duration of the reproductive period and life span under the influence of the demographic trend towards an increase in age at first birth in modern developed countries.
Acting as a factor of artificial selection, increase parental age may lead to a shift of the upper boundary of the reproductive period in the new generations, and life expectancy.
The article discusses the role of a number of social factors to identify trends.
