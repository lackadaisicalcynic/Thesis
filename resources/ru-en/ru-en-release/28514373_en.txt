[Acute otitis media in the children: etiology and the problems of antibacterial therapy].  The objective of the present review was to summarize the currently available literature data on etiology of acute otitis media in the children, the role of biofilms in the development of this pathology, and sensitivity of its principal causative factors to various antibiotic medications.
The secondary objective was to elaborate the practical guidelines for the prevention of acute otitis media in the children.
