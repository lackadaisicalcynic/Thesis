[International multicenter studies of treatment of severe traumatic brain injury].  Despite the introduction of new diagnostic and therapeutic methods, traumatic brain injury (TBI) remains one of the leading cause of death and disability worldwide.
Standards and recommendations on conservative and surgical treatment of TBI patients should be based on concepts and methods with proven efficacy.
The authors present a review of studies of the treatment and surgery of severe TBI: DECRA, RESCUEicp, STITCH(TRAUMA), CRASH, CRASH-2, CAPTAIN, NABIS: H ll, Eurotherm 3235.
Important recommendations of the international group IMPACT are considered.
