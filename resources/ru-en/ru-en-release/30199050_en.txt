[Influence of anastomotic leakage on the long-term outcomes in patients with colorectal cancer].  Anastomotic leakage after surgery for colorectal cancer is a widely known factor aggravating immediate outcomes.
At the same time, deterioration of oncological results is under much less attention.
Long-term consequences of anastomotic leakage and possible mechanism of negative effect of this complication on long-term results are reviewed in the article.
