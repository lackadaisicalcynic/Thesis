[A new method of treatment of Korsakoff's (amnestic) psychosis: neurostimulation-correction of the sympathetic nervous system].
AIM  To provide a rationale for a new method of neurostimulation - correction of the sympathetic nervous system based on the evolution of the electrical stimulation of the brain as a component of neurorehabilitation.
MATERIAL AND METHODS
Authors treated 16 patients with Korsakoff's (amnestic) psychosis using this method.  RESULTS
Positive results were obtained in all patients.
A typical case is described.  CONCLUSION
The proposed method has a high therapeutic potential.
