[Intracranial neurenteric cysts: experience of the Burdenko Neurosurgical Institute in the XXIth century].  We present a series of cases of a rare pathology, intracranial neuroenteric cysts, a review of the international literature, and the experience in treating this pathology.
MATERIAL AND METHODS
Seven patients with intracranial neuroenteric cysts underwent surgery at the Neurosurgical Institute in the period between 2000 and 2015.
CONCLUSION
The main and only technique for treatment of intracranial neurenteric cysts is their resection.
