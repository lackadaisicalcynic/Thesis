[Screening tools for frailty in ambulatory care].  The article gives a brief description of the frailty syndrome and comprehensive geriatric assessment.
We describe two classical models - a model of the phenotype and the index of the frailty.
The basic questionnaires for frailty syndrome screening in outpatient practice, as well as research on the validation of these questionnaires are presented.
The results of comparative studies of questionnaires for frailty screening are shown.
