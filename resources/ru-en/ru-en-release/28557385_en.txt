[Telomere-telomerase system in aging, norm and pathology (literature review)].  This literature review presents results of research showing association between functional activity of the telomere-telomerase system and mental cognitive and emotional processes in normal and various pathological states: chronic stress, depression, bipolar disorder, schizophrenia, mild cognitive impairment and dementia in aging.
It also refers to age-specific, psycho-social, economic, immunological, genetic and epigenetic factors that influence these relationships.
