[Forensic medical diagnosis and conclusions in the case of nose bone fracture with blood aspiration into the respiratory tract].  The authors consider the variants of the formulation of forensic medical diagnosis and expert conclusions in the cases of death among the victims of nasal bone fractures and blood aspiration proposed by different researchers.
They emphasize the inadmissibility of the formal logical approach in such situations and the necessity of pathogenetic analysis of each concrete case for the elucidation of nosological forms and syndromes responsible for the fatal outcome.
