[Long-term results of the program «Point of Sobriety» in patients with alcohol dependence in the Krasnodar region].
AIM
To analyze the long-term results of the «Point of Sobriety» program in patients with alcohol dependence.
MATERIAL AND METHODS
It was analyzed the results of treatment of 104 patients treated 2 or more years before the current study.  RESULTS AND CONCLUSION
The high efficacy of complex treatment with naltrexone injections was demonstrated.
Remission during more than 1 year was achieved in 64.4% of patients, more than 2 years in 45.2%.  Most patients reported improvement in social and family status.
