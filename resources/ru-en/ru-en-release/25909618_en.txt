[Dental status in children and adolescents diagnosed with obesity].  Dental status was studied in 168 obese teenagers aged from 12 to 17 years.
The study revealed high prevalence in this group of teenagers of oral pathology like caries and fluorosis, inflammatory periodontal disease in 75% of children and poor oral hygiene level.
One should consider high need for development of treatment-and-prophylactic measures for improvement of dental status in obese teenagers.
