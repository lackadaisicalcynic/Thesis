[Accuracy of the choice of the dependence syndrome treatment: evidence-based addictology against clinical practice].
In article it is analyzed the reasons of discrepancy of arguments at a choice of dependence syndrome treatment in Russian, in particular when forming National clinical protocols.  The conclusion that rejection of use principles of evidence-based medicine is based on the objective and subjective reasons is drawn.
