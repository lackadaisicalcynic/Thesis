[Lo-back pain and compression-ischemic neuropathies: strategies for diagnosis, therapy and rehabilitation].
The article discusses such a common pathology as pain syndrome in the lower back and limbs.  A variety of etiological causes, anatomical features leading to the formation of this persistent algic disorder lead to therapeutic failures in clinical practice.
The authors consider in detail the most common types of compression-ischemic neuropathy accompanying back pain, suggest diagnostic algorithms and practical recommendations.
Results of foreign, Russian and own research are presented.
