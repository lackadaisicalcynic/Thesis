[Diagnostic imaging for sclerochoroidal calcifica-tion].  The article presents two clinical cases of sclerochoroidal calcification.
This is a rare benign condition that usually does not display itself and is only occasionally diagnosed.
It can also be accompanied by systemic disorder of mineral metabolism.  The data obtained through medical imaging indicate partial destruction of the choroid at the site of the damage that causes secondary changes in the overlying retina.
