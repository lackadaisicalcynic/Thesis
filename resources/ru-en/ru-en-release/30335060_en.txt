[A case of solid pseudopapillary tumor of the pancreas: features of the course, difficulty in diagnosis].  Solid pseudopapillary tumor is a low-grade malignant neoplasm of the pancreas.
The clinical manifestations are variable, ranging from an asymptomatic course to cases with severe symptoms that dramatically impair the patients' status.
The paper describes the rare case of a solid pseudopapillary tumor in a 34-year-old woman, which was accompanied by difficulties in the interpretation of clinical data and morphological patterns.
