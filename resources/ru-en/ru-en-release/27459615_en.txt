[Subcutaneous panniculitis-like T-cell lymphoma: The authors' experience of diagnosis and treatment].  The paper gives the data of clinical, histological, immunohistochemical, and molecular studies and the results of positron emission tomography in 3 cases of subcutaneous panniculitis-like T-cell lymphoma (SPTCL).
It shows the high efficiency of a GEM-P regimen in the treatment of patients with SPTCL.
