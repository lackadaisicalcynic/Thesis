[Combined surgical treatment for bullous keratopathy with concomitant uncompensated glaucoma].  The article discusses a clinical case of combined surgical treatment for painful bullous keratopathy in an eye with uncompensated far-advanced glaucoma.  Ex-PRESS Glaucoma Filtration Device was used for anterior chamber drainage.
The procedure was supplemented by amniotic membrane implantation under the scleral flap, microdiathermocoagulation of large corneal epithelial bullae, and epikeratoplasty.
