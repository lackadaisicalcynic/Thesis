[Congenital cysts and fistulas on the neck].  This paper presents the currently available data on congenital lateral cysts and fistulas of the neck based on the authors' original experience and the review of the relevant literature.
The modern methods for diagnostics and the surgical treatment of these conditions are described and the causes underling the development of their relapses are analyzed.
Recommendations for the prevention of congenital cysts and fistulas on the neck are proposed.
