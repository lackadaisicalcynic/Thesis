[Laparoscopic surgery for colon cancer].
AIM
To assess laparoscopic surgery in treatment of colon cancer patients.  MATERIAL AND METHODS
The results of laparoscopic treatment of patients with colorectal cancer are presented in the article.  It was estimated the influence of various clinical parameters including age, gender, comorbidities, tumor localization and stage and complications on laparoscopic management of these patients.
CONCLUSION
It was revealed that efficiency of laparoscopic surgery in patients with colon cancer is affected by tumor stage and presence of complications.
