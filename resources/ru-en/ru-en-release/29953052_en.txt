[The importance of the quantitative assessment of the hearing impairment in the subjects employed under conditions of enhanced noise].  The article describes the domestic modern diagnostic and expert approaches to the quantitative assessment of the hearing impairment in the subjects employed under conditions of enhanced occupational noise.
It is concluded that the quantitative criteria for the determination of the severity of hearing loss in the workers of the 'noisy' professions adopted in this country are comparable with the respective international standards of and approaches to the medico-social examination practiced worldwide.
