[Symptoms of obsessive-compulsive disorder in neurological diseases].  Obsessive-compulsive disorder (OCD) is a common form of neurosis.  Symptoms of OCD could develop as a sign of focal brain lesion, particularly in multiple sclerosis, extrapyramidal disorders, epilepsy, less frequently - in other diseases.
Timely diagnosis and treatment of the symptoms of OCD is an important aspect in the management of mentioned neurological disorders.
