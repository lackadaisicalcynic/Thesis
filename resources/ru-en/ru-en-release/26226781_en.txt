[Liposarcoma of the spermatic cord].  The paper reviews the literature on primary paratesticular tumors.
It describes the classification, brief characteristics of liposarcomas and the morphological pattern of the tumor with an immunohistochemical profile.
A clinical case of differentiated liposarcoma of the spermatic cord with myxoid and rhabdomyoblastic differentiation is depicted in a 61-year-old man.
