[Audiodiagnostics in examination of patients with internal TMJ derangement].  The aim of the study was to assess the efficacy of audiodiagnostics in dysfunctional TMJ disorders.
Thirty-four patients were included in the study.
The analysis of phonograms revealed correlation of adverse sound effects with varying degrees of internal TMJ-disorder seen by MRI imaging.  The study results confirm the advantages of electronic registration of TMJ sound phenomena.
