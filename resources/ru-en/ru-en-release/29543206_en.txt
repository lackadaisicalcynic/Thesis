[Mechanical dilation of the pupil and its possible applications in femtosecond laser-assisted cataract surgery].  In certain cases intraoperative pupil dilation during cataract surgery may be necessary to improve the visualization of intraocular structures and assure the atraumatic nature of surgical manipulations.
The article reviews a wide range of pupil expanders, their historical aspects and possibilities of their application in modern cataract surgery.
