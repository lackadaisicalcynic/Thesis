[Neuroanatomical age-related changes in apathy].  The introduction of new instrumental methods, in particular neuroimaging techniques, in academic studies and clinical practice allowed to identify neuroanatomical and functional correlates of different clinical syndromes.
Authors summarized the results of magnetic-resonance tomography (MRI) and one-photon emission computed tomography (OPECT) studies of apathy of old-age developed in the structure of different neurological and mental diseases.  The literature over that last two decades was reviewed
