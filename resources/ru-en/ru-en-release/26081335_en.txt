[A case of recurrent Devic's opticomyelitis].  A brief literature review on autoimmune inflammatory-demyelinating CNS disease - Devic's opticomyelitis, which is rare in Caucasians, is presented.
We report a case of this disease.
Epidemiological issues, differential diagnosis of opticomyelitis from multiple sclerosis and other non-demyelinating diseases are considered.
Common strategies of patient management and perspectives of development of specific treatment of opticomyelitis are outlined.
