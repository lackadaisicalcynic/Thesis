[The season of birth and mortality (Based on workers mortality epidemiological study materials)].  The article presents a comparative assessment of the death absolute risk for the humans born in different seasons and months of the year based on the epidemiological studies found on the retrospective cohort for the 40 years period (the end of the 20th-beginning 21st century).
It is shown that mortality of people born in January-February differ from similar parameters of persons born in March-April.
